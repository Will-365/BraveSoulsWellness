About
Brave Souls Wellness

Brave Souls Wellness Group Ltd is a registered wellness company based in Kigali, Rwanda. We provide structured fitness and wellness coaching that develops not only physical strength, but also mindset, discipline, and inner stability — because lasting transformation starts from within.

We are founded and led by a husband-and-wife leadership team committed to building disciplined wellness systems that develop both body and spirit. Our approach blends professional fitness coaching with character development, accountability, and holistic wellness practices.
